Just another STT captions and translation tool. And a bot.

Select the language you speak, and the language you want to translate to (disabled by default). When you're done, click on Start Captions to open the captions overlay page, which you can then set as your OBS overlay source.

Jabberer doesn't require mod rights. To stop Jabberer, simply kick it from your channel. There's really no other way. I'm sure it won't hold a grudge.
