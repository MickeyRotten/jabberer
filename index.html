Welcome to Jabberer, the next best STT solution for streamers! Select the language you speak, and if you want to translate it to another language. When you're done, click on Start Captions to open the captions overlay page, which you can then set as your OBS overlay source.

If you invite Jabberer to your Twitch channel, it can send your captions also to your chat! Click the button below to authorize Jabberer to read and send messages. It can also read channel redeems for future features.
